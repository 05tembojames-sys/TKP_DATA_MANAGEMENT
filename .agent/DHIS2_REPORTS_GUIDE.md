1. **Top Bar**: White background with DHIS2 blue accent
2. **Sidebar**: List-based navigation (left side)
3. **Main Content**: White cards with table views
4. **Typography**: Roboto font family

### **Components**
- **Buttons**: Rounded corners (4px), DHIS2 blue primary
- **Tables**: Clean, bordered, sticky headers
- **Forms**: Consistent padding, clear labels
- **Cards**: White background, subtle shadow
